Teacher's Corner

New Teacher - meet Jill!

Jill Clary, RYT, began practicing yoga in 2003 when weekly classes were offered through her workplace. She developed a regular yoga practice both at home and in local studios and in 2015 completed the Schoolhouse Yoga Teacher Training program. She is a firm believer that yoga is for everyone and her classes, combining different yoga styles such as Ashtanga, Kundalini, and Yin, along with breath work and meditation, move from slow to a challenging flow. She welcomes you to her classes.
Jill teaches the 9:30am and the 6:30pm Yoga 1 classes on Tuesdays at our North Hills school.

Workshops

Family Yoga Workshops with Bailey Zembower

In our family yoga workshop, we will focus on fun and playful movement as well as calming breath work to bring the family together in a lighthearted practice. We will move through a physical asana practice designed to be mindful of the bodies and minds of children and teenagers. Join us to unite your practice with your family as we include a brief guided meditation to help to focus busy minds of children and parents alike. Family members and friends of young yogis (ages 4 and over) are welcome.

Sunday, January 8 | 2-3pm | $10/family member | Squirrel Hill

Meditation Workshop with Joni Sturgill

Start a meditation practice, or revive and deepen your practice while setting positive intentions for the new year. In this workshop, Joni will review meditation basics and also discuss methods to invite in peace and clarity for the new year. You'll learn to work toward overcoming the obstacles that can sometimes prevent you from meditating, plus, you'll learn techniques to bring mindful awareness into your everyday life. Class will end with a long, guided relaxation.

Sunday, January 8 | 3-5pm | $35 | East Liberty

Moon Salutations with Kendell Romanelli

Moon Salutations are a flowing yoga practice designed to draw the energy of the body, mind and heart, inward to a more reflective nature honoring the energy of the full moon. In this workshop we will combine the Moon Salutation sequence with mantras, meditations and restorative postures. We will use the practice of candle gazing and a guided deep relaxation to clear the mind and calm the soul. It is a time to heal and reflect on all the beauty within us and around us.

Prenatal Partner Yoga Workshop with Brenda Maser

Moms to be! Bring your partners, a family member or a friend to share a fun prenatal yoga experience where you will learn supportive partner poses, breath work, massage and relaxation techniques designed to help you connect with one another and to prepare for the arrival of your little one into the world! All stages of pregnancy and levels of experience are welcome.

Sunday, January 15 | 1:30-3pm | $35/couple | Squirrel Hill

Meditation Workshop for Teachers with Joni Sturgill

Whether you are a yoga teacher looking to incorporate meditation into your classes, a school teacher looking to teach students to relax and focus, a human resources professional looking to help employees reduce stress, or a parent wanting to help your children relax and learn the lifelong skill of focused meditation, join Joni in this course to experience basic meditations and learn how to break them down and teach them to others. There will be time for practice, lecture and questions. A handout will be emailed after the course.

Sunday, January 15 | 2-5pm | $40 | East Liberty

Women's Healing Circle with Kendell Romanelli

This class is designed to use the energy of yoga, mantra and meditation to heal the body, mind and soul. It is open to women of all ages and backgrounds. When we come together in a circle, the power of the group helps to support and uplift all the members of the group. We will begin class with some yoga exercises and stretches. We will then light a candle and speak an intention for healing in five words or less. We will support our intention by chanting a healing mantra and close with a deep relaxation supported by the healing power of guided meditation.

Friday, January 27 | 6:30-8pm | $10 | North Hills

Sitting is Killing Me: Yoga to Undo the Damage of Your Desk Job

Do you sit at a keyboard for hours? Do you have tight hips and legs? Neck, shoulder and back pain and/or discomfort? Camping out all day at a desk can also create an unhealthy posture - that you take with you when you leave for the day - the back and shoulders hunch down and the neck protrudes forward. Join Jill Clary, RYT, for a practice designed to relieve tension and lesson the chronic ache of office work. We'll start with a chair practice we can do while at our desk or on our commute, move to our mat for a floor practice concentrating on these key areas, and finish with restorative/yin poses to leave us feeling renewed and recharged.

Saturday, January 28 | 2-4pm | $25 | North Hills
