Teacher's Corner

Kara has been a yoga student for over 10 years. In March of 2015, she decided to deepen her own personal practice by enrolling in yoga teacher training through Schoolhouse Yoga. This decision has ignited a passion within her to share her love of yoga with others. Prior to becoming a yoga instructor, Kara worked for many years within the social service field. She developed the skill of being able to hold space for others through her extensive experience as a social worker. She brings this skill to every one of her classes. The core philosophy of Karaâ€™s classes is that everyone is capable to feel strong and empowered in their own bodies. Her classes focus on dynamic movements, breath work, and proper alignment in order to guide students down a path of self-discovery. Kara creates a supportive environment for students through clear, creative, compassionate and enthusiastic guidance.

Sitting is Killing Me: Yoga to Undo the Damage of Your Desk Job

Do you sit at a keyboard for hours? Do you have tight hips and legs? Neck, shoulder and back pain and/or discomfort? Camping out all day at a desk can also create an unhealthy posture - that you take with you when you leave for the day - the back and shoulders hunch down and the neck protrudes forward. Join Jill Clary, RYT, for a practice designed to relieve tension and lesson the chronic ache of office work. We'll start with a chair practice we can do while at our desk or on our commute, move to our mat for a floor practice concentrating on these key areas, and finish with restorative/yin poses to leave us feeling renewed and recharged.

Moon Salutations with Kendell Romanelli

Moon Salutations are a flowing yoga practice designed to draw the energy of the body, mind and heart, inward to a more reflective nature honoring the energy of the full moon. In this workshop we will combine the Moon Salutation sequence with mantras, meditations and restorative postures. We will use the practice of candle gazing and a guided deep relaxation to clear the mind and calm the soul. It is a time to heal and reflect on all the beauty within us and around us.
