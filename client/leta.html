About Leta

Leta Koontz, RYT, began her study of Ashtanga Yoga with Lori Brungard in 1998. She has trained at the Ashtanga Yoga Research Institute in Mysore, India, and has completed teacher trainings with the Himalayan Institute and with Tim Miller. In 2002, she founded Schoolhouse Yoga which now has three locations in Pittsburgh. Leta enjoys challenging her students while keeping the practice fun and playful.
